Our Story

Built by traders, for traders

QuantMetrics was born from frustration. After watching countless traders fail prop firm challenges with unvalidated strategies, we realized the industry had a massive gap.

Existing tools were either too complex (requiring Python knowledge) or too expensive ($250+/month). Meanwhile, 95% of traders were testing strategies in Excel or just winging it with real money.

We spent 6 months building the platform we wish existed: no-code strategy testing with proprietary metrics (ESI, PVS) that reveal genuine edge. Our goal? Help 10,000+ traders pass prop firm challenges with validated strategies.

Today, QuantMetrics analyzes millions in trading volume, helping traders worldwide make data-driven decisions before risking capital.
