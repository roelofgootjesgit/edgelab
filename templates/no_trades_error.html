Why This Happened

This is normal and not an error. It simply means your strategy conditions were too strict for the selected market period, or the market conditions didn't align with your setup.

What You Can Do

  • Relax Your Conditions Try using fewer Decision Blocks or less strict sub-confirmations. Start with 1-2 blocks and add more if needed.
  • Extend the Test Period Use a longer period (3mo or 6mo instead of 1mo) to give your strategy more opportunities to match.
  • Remove Session Filter If you're filtering by session, try removing it to allow trades during all market hours.
  • Adjust Parameters Review your indicator parameters (periods, thresholds) - they might be too restrictive for current market conditions.
  • Try Different Market/Timeframe Some strategies work better on different markets or timeframes. Experiment with alternatives.
