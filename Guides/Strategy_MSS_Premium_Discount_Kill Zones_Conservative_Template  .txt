**SHORT Entry Conditions:**
```yaml

  - âœ“ In Kill Zone (London OR New York)
  - âœ“ Bearish MSS detected
  - âœ“ Price in Premium zone (top 38.2% of range)
```

### Exit Rules


### Module Parameters

**Market Structure Shift:**
```python
{
    'swing_lookback': 5,           # Candles to identify swing points
    'break_threshold_pct': 0.2,    # 0.2% beyond swing for confirmation
    'confirmation_candles': 2      # Wait 2 candles to confirm break
}
```

**Premium/Discount Zones:**
```python
{
    'lookback_candles': 50,        # Range calculation period
    'premium_threshold': 0.618,    # Top 38.2% = Premium
    'discount_threshold': 0.382,   # Bottom 38.2% = Discount
    'equilibrium_range': 0.1       # Middle 20% = Equilibrium
```

### For Manual Trading

**Daily Routine:**
```
1. Check time: Only trade during Kill Zones
   - London: 07:00-10:00 UTC
   - New York: 12:00-15:00 UTC

2. Open XAUUSD 15m chart

3. Check Premium/Discount:
   - Calculate 50-candle high/low
   - Determine if price in Premium, Discount, or Equilibrium
   - Only look for trades in Premium (SHORT) or Discount (LONG)

4. Wait for MSS:
   - Downtrend breaks above previous swing high = Bullish MSS
   - Uptrend breaks below previous swing low = Bearish MSS

5. Enter trade:
   - MSS + correct zone + kill zone = ENTER
   - Entry: Next candle after MSS
   - SL: 10 points away
   - TP: 15 points away

6. Manage:
   - No adjusting stops
   - Let trade hit TP or SL
   - Max hold: ~7 hours (30 candles)
```
